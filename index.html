My Project 1

Neubrutalism is a web design style that combines the rough brutalism with a modern touch. Its characteristics include high-contrast colors, thick borders, bold typography, and simple yet functional layouts. This style is often used for portfolio websites and digital products that want to be unique and bold.

My Project 2

Glassmorphism is a web design style that features a transparent glass effect with blur, often combined with soft colors and glowing elements. Its characteristics include high transparency, blur effects, soft borders, and soft shadows to create a modern and futuristic look. This style is often used in modern UI/UX, especially in dashboards, applications, and technology websites.

My Project 3

Retro is a web design style inspired by the aesthetics of the past, such as the 80s and 90s. Its characteristics include bright colors, grainy effects, vintage typography, and graphic elements such as pixel art or vintage illustrations. This style is often used for nostalgia-themed websites, classic games, and brands with unique identities.

Neon is a web design style inspired by neon lights with bright colors and glowing light effects. Its characteristics include dark backgrounds, high contrast colors, glow effects, and futuristic typography. This style is often used for cyberpunk, technology, and nightlife themed websites.
